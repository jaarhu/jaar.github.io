Axelby Outstanding Paper Award of IEEE TAC in 2006. Currently Professor at the Systems Engineering and Automatic Control Department at the University of Seville (Spain) and Head of the [Multi-Robot And Control Systems (MACS)](https://investigacion.us.es/sisius/sis_depgrupos.php?ct=&cs=&seltext=TEP-995&selfield=CodPAI) research group. CNRS Visitor (France) since 2005 and Academic Visitor at Imperial College London (UK) in 2008-09-10 and 2011. He has served on the editorial boards of various scientific journals. Main interest in Nonlinear Control Theory & Applications with emphasis on Mechanical, Aerospace and Robotic systems.
